# Power BI enterprise deployment whitepaper

Deploying Power BI in a large enterprise is a complex task that requires a lot of thought and planning. The purpose of the Enterprise Deployment whitepaper is to help make your Power BI deployment a success: it covers key considerations, the decisions which will be necessary throughout the process, and potential issues you may encounter. Best practices and suggestions are offered when possible.

The target audience for this whitepaper is technology professionals. Some knowledge of Power BI and general business intelligence concepts is assumed.
