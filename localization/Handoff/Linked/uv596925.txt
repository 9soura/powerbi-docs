	<title>QuickBooks Online content pack for Power BI</title>
	
	When you connect to your QuickBooks Online data from Power BI you immediately get a Power BI dashboard and Power BI reports that provide insights about your business cash flow, profitability, customers, and more. Use the dashboard and reports as they are, or customize them to highlight the information you care most about. The data is refreshed automatically once a day.<br><br>Connect to the <a href="https://dxt.powerbi.com/getdata/services/quickbooks-online">QuickBooks Online content pack</a> for Power BI.<br><br><b>Note:</b> If you're having issues connecting, see the <b>Troubleshooting</b> section at the end of this article.<br><br><span><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/81213732/PBI_QBO_Dash.png"></span><br><br><b>Important</b>: To import your QuickBooks Online data into Power BI, you need to be an admin on your QuickBooks Online account and sign in with your admin account credentials.<br><br><ol><li style="vertical-align: middle;" value="1"><font face="Calibri"><span style="line-height: 14.66px; font-size: 14.66px;">Select&nbsp;<span style="font-weight: bolder;">Get Data</span>&nbsp;at the bottom of the left navigation pane.</span></font><br></li><br><font face="Calibri"><span style="line-height: 1.4em; font-size: 11pt;">&nbsp;<img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/82642324/PBI_GetData.png"></span></font><br><br><li><font face="Calibri"><span style="line-height: 20.53px; font-size: 14.66px;">In the&nbsp;<span style="font-weight: bolder;">Services&nbsp;</span>box, select&nbsp;<span style="font-weight: bolder;">Get</span>.</span></font><br><br><font face="Calibri"><span style="line-height: 1.4em; font-size: 11pt;"><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/82642363/PBI_GetServices.png">&nbsp;<br><br></span></font></li><li>Select&nbsp;<b>QuickBooks Online</b>, then select <b>Connect</b>.<br><br><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/82654423/PBI_QBO_Connect.png"></span><br><br></li><li>Select <b>oAuth2</b> for Authentication Method and select <b>Sign In</b>.&nbsp;<br><br></li><li>When prompted, enter your QuickBooks Online credentials and follow the QuickBooks Online authentication process. If you are already signed in to QuickBooks Online in your browser, you may not be prompted for credentials.<br><span style="line-height: 1.4em;"><b>Note: </b>You need admin credentials for your QuickBooks Online account.<br><br></span></li><li>Select the company you would like to connect to Power BI in the next screen.<br><br><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/81220110/PBI_QBO_Almost.png"></span><br><br></li><li>Select <b>Authorize</b> in the next screen to begin the import process. This can take a few minutes depending on the size of your company data.&nbsp;<br><br><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/81220500/PBI_QBO_AuthorizeSm.png"></span><br><br><span style="line-height: 1.4em;">After Power BI imports the data, you see a new dashboard, report, and dataset in the left navigation pane. New items are marked with a yellow asterisk *.<br><br></span><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/82657396/PBI_QBO_LeftNavNew.png"></span><br><br></li><li>Select the QuickBooks Online dashboard. This is the dashboard Power BI created automatically to display your imported data. You can modify this dashboard to display your data any way you want.&nbsp;<br><br><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/81216210/PBI_QBO_Dash.png"></span><br><br></li><li>What can you do now?</li></ol><blockquote><ul><li>Rename the dashboard: Select the ellipsis (...) next to the QuickBook Online dashboard &gt; <b>Rename</b>.<br><br></li><li>Try <a href="https://support.powerbi.com/knowledgebase/articles/474566-q-a-in-power-bi-preview">asking a question in the Q&amp;A box</a> at the top of the dashboard.<br><br></li><li><a href="https://support.powerbi.com/knowledgebase/articles/424878">Change the tiles</a> in the dashboard.</li><li>Select a tile to <a href="https://support.powerbi.com/knowledgebase/articles/425669">open the underlying report</a>.&nbsp;</li></ul></blockquote><h3><br>Troubleshooting&nbsp;</h3><div><span style="line-height: 19.6px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;"><br></span><span style="line-height: 19.6px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;"><b>“Oops!
