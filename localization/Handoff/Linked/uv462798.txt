	<title>Zendesk content pack for Power BI</title>
	
	To analyze your Zendesk data in Power BI, you must connect to Zendesk using your Zendesk <b>admin </b>account
 credentials so that Power BI will be able to import&nbsp; your data.&nbsp; You will get a Power BI
 dashboard and a set of Power BI reports that provide insights about your
 ticket volumes and agent performance. You can use the dashboard and reports
 provided, or customize them to highlight the information you care most
 about.&nbsp; The data will be refreshed
 automatically once a day.&nbsp;<br><br>Connect to the <a href="https://app.powerbi.com/getdata/services/zendesk">Zendesk content pack</a> for Power BI.<br>
