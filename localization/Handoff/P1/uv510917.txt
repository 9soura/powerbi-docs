	<title>Get started with the Power BI for Windows app</title>

	<p><span style="line-height: 1.4em;" abp="32">The&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 19.6px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">Microsoft Power BI</span><span style="line-height: 19.6px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">&nbsp;for&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 1.4em;">Windows app preview&nbsp;adds to the&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 19.6px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">Power </span><span style="line-height: 1.4em;">BI experience. It provides live, touch-enabled&nbsp;mobile access to your important business information, so you can view and interact with your company dashboards and reports easily, from anywhere. Explore the data in your dashboards, and share them with your colleagues in email.</span></p><p><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/80237787/PBI_WinAppHomeSmall.png"></span><br></p><p>Bring your data together&nbsp;by creating&nbsp;<a href="http://support.powerbi.com/knowledgebase/articles/430814-get-started-with-power-bi" abp="42">dashboards and reports in Power BI</a>.
