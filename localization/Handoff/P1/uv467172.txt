	<title>Get started with the iPad app (Power BI for iOS)</title>

	<span style="line-height: 1.4em;">The iPad app for Microsoft Power BI for iOS&nbsp;brings&nbsp;the mobile BI experience to Power BI. With live, touch-enabled&nbsp;mobile access to your important business information, you can view and interact with your company dashboards and reports easily, from anywhere. Explore the data in your dashboards, and share them with your colleagues in email or text messages.</span><br><div><br></div><div><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/80592789/PBI_iPad_DashTile.png"></span></div><p abp="100">Maybe a colleague sent you a link to a dashboard? Now you can view it on your iPad.</p><div><span style="line-height: 1.4em;">Or bring your own data together and create&nbsp;</span><a style="font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;" href="http://support.powerbi.com/knowledgebase/articles/430814-get-started-with-power-bi">dashboards and reports in Power BI</a><span style="line-height: 1.4em;">. Then interact with your dashboards and reports, explore the data in them, and share snapshots with the iPad app for Power BI.</span></div><h2>Download the iOS app for the iPad</h2><div><br></div><div><a href="http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkId=522062">Download the iPad app</a> from the Apple App Store.<br></div><h2>Sign up and sign&nbsp;in</h2><div><br>Go to <a href="http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkID=513879">Power BI to sign up</a>&nbsp;for the service. Now you have a place to create dashboards to bring your data together. Then sign&nbsp;in to Power BI from your iPad, to see your own dashboards from anywhere.</div><ol><li>Tap <a href="http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkID=513879">Sign up</a> to create a Power BI account.<br>Start <a href="http://support.powerbi.com/knowledgebase/articles/430814-get-started-with-power-bi">creating your own dashboards and reports</a>.</li><li>Back&nbsp;in the iPad app, tap <a href="http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkId=522061">Sign in</a> to view your dashboards and reports.</li></ol><h2>Get started with samples</h2><p>Even without signing up or signing in, you can play with the samples. After you download the app, you can view the samples or sign in. Go back to the samples whenever you want from the dashboards home page.</p><ul><li>Tap <b>see our samples</b>, then pick a role and explore the sample dashboard for that role.<br><span></span><span></span><span><span></span><br><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/76050548/PBI_iPad_Samples2.png"></span><br><br>NOTE: Not all features are available in the samples.
